# Example Spotify voice commands

These are example phrases for the Spotify/PTT bridge. Actual behavior depends on the local config, active Spotify device, and available playlists.

## Playback

- play
- pause
- next song
- previous song
- restart song
- mute
- volume 35

## Playlists

- switch to chill playlist
- play my gym playlist
- put on focus

Playlist aliases belong in local `config.json`, not in the public repo.
